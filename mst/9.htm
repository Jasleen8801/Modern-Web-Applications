CSS Box Model

      It considers every HTML element to be a rectangular box.
      

      It consists of 4 parts:
      a) Content
      b) Padding
      c) Border
      d) Margin
      

      Content: It is an area  where text and images appear.
      Padding: area between the border and content.
      Border: is an area that goes around the Padding and the content
      Margin: It is an empty space around your element used to separate it from its neighboring elements.
